Welcome to BOBino!

A low cost environmental sensing platform for Arduino.

BOBino continues the work of the Elementary Basic Observational Buoy by providing open source, low cost electric data capture sensors to elementary students and educators in Charleston, the state of South Carolina, and beyond!

BOBino is a reference design that uses Arduino off the shelf hardware including: Arduino Uno development board, SD card shield, and a variety of inexpensive sensors and power supplies. The assembled unit records data to an SD card, floating on an eBOB, and provides students the opportunity to perform environmental data capture, analysis, charting, and statistics.
